About Us

Operating since 1992, Kings Group is one of the largest real estate companies in Hyderabad city established by Mr Shanawaz with a vision to convert people's dreams into reality. The real strength of the Kings Group of Companies lies in the young, energetic and entrepreneurial generation of promoters in the three sons of Mr Shanawaz.

For the past 29 years we have successfully completed many real estate projects covering residential and commercial purposes.

We aim to assist in making the best real estate decisions based on customer needs within the legalised manner with permissions from authorities like GHMC /HMDA for layouts on residential and commercial projects.
